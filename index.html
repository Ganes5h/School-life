 Student Life

       Shri Shivaji Vidyalay is a school that provides a rich and fulfilling student life experience for its students. The school has a strong academic program that is complemented by a variety of extracurricular activities that allow students to pursue their interests and passions. In addition to classroom learning, students have access to a range of resources such as the school library, etc.Shri Shivaji Vidyalay encourages students to participate in cultural and social events such as annual day celebrations, festivals, and charity events. With a focus on holistic development, the school promotes personal growth, community service, and leadership opportunities for all its students. Overall, the student life experience at Shri Shivaji Vidyalay is designed to be engaging, dynamic, and fulfilling, allowing students to thrive and achieve their full potential.

 Academic Life

     Thri Shivaji Vidhyalay is a vibrant school that provides students with a well-rounded education. The academic life at Shri Shivaji Vidhyalaya is challenging and stimulating. The school follows a comprehensive curriculum that emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical application.

 NCC

     In addition to the extracurricular activities, students at Shri Shivaji Vidhyalaya can also join the National Cadet Corps (NCC), which is a youth organization that provides military training, leadership development, and community service, opportunities. The NCC program at the school is highly respected, and many students have gone on to excel in the armed forces.

 Picnic
